General

Taekwon-Do is a Korean martial art and therefore all terminology is based on the Korean language

  • The Founder of Taekwon-Do: General Choi Hong Hi (9th November 1918 - 15th June 2002)
  • Taekwon-Do was founded on: 11th April 1955
  • International Taekwon-Do Federation was founded on: 22nd March 1966

Meaning of Taekwon-Do

  • Tae: to break or destroy with the foot
  • Kwon: to break or destroy with the hand
  • Do: the way, method, or art of doing something
  • Taekwon-Do: the art of hand and foot fighting

Grading

There are 10 kup grades in Taekwon-Do represented by the colour belts.

There are 9 dan degrees in Taekwondo represented by the black belt with Roman numerals. 9th dan is the highest rank in Taekwon-Do.

Sets of fundamental exercises practiced by beginners.

Blocking: Makgi

Middle level inner forearm block: Kaunde An Palmok Magki (Blocking arm starts UNDER the reaction arm).

Low level outer forearm block: Najunde Bakat Palmok Makgi (Blocking arm starts ON TOP of the reaction arm).

Student Oath

It is recited at the start of official events.

Voi respecta principiile Taekwon-Do

Voi respecta Instructorii și Seniorii

Nu voi folosi în alte scopuri Taekwon-Do

Voi fi campion al Libertății și al Dreptății

Voi contribui la construirea unei lumi pașnice

I shall observe the tenets of Taekwon-Do

I shall respect my instructors and seniors

I shall never misuse Taekwon-Do

I shall be a champion of freedom and justice

I shall build a more peaceful world.
