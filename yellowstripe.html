General & Important Dates

  • The Founder of Taekwon-Do: General Choi Hong Hi (9th November 1918 - 15th June 2002)
  • Taekwon-Do was founded on: 11th April 1955
  • International Taekwon-Do Federation was founded on: 22nd March 1966

Meaning of Taekwon-Do

  • Tae: to break or destroy with the foot
  • Kwon: to break or destroy with the hand
  • Do: the way, method, or art of doing something
  • Taekwon-Do: the art of hand and foot fighting
There are 10 kup grades in Taekwon-Do represented by the colour belts. There are 9 dan degrees in Taekwondo represented by the black belt with Roman numerals. 9th dan is the highest rank in Taekwon-Do.
